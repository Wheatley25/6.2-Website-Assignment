About us

Welcome to Sir Crunch. Sir Crunch is a restaurant that serves high quality food such as
pizzas, burgers and hot dogs for cheap prices that come with a side of your choice from
the large variety of sides that we have to offer. If you are thinking about eating with us,
you should consider visiting the menu page so you are able to decide if what we serve
appeals to you and so you are able to calculate how much you will have to spend.
If you would like to order from us you can choose to either takeout or eat in our
restaurant and you can only order in a Sir Crunch restaurant.
